About Ashya

At Ashya, we’re passionate about creating unique handmade ceramics that blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design. Each piece is carefully crafted to bring warmth and beauty into your home and life.

Founded in Hamburg, Germany, our studio focuses on quality, sustainability, and the art of storytelling through ceramics. We offer workshops, custom pieces, and an open studio where creativity comes to life. Join us in celebrating the tactile beauty of clay and the magic of handmade art.
