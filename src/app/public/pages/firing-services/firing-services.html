Firing Service

Do you create your own ceramics and need a place to fire them? At Ashya Art, you can either rent the entire kiln (80l or 90 l) or arrange for the firing of just a few pieces.

Please ensure you bring details about the clay and glazes used — without this information we cannot accept your ceramics, as they must be fired at the correct temperatures.

Costs
  • • Renting Kiln (80l) for the bisk firing – 35€
  • • Renting Kiln (90l) for the bisk firing – 40€
  • • Renting Kiln (90l) or the glaze firing – 45€
  • • Firing one piece only (size of the cup) bisk or glaze – 4€

Make an appointment to bring your ceramics for firing.
