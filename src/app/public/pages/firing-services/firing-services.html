Firing Service

Do you create your own ceramics and need a place to fire them? At Ashya Art, you can either rent the entire kiln (80 l) or arrange for the firing of just a few pieces.

Make an appointment to bring your ceramics for firing. Please ensure you bring details about the clay and glazes used — without this information we cannot accept your ceramics, as they must be fired at the correct temperatures.

Costs
  • • Renting kiln (80 l) for bisque firing – 35€
  • • Renting kiln (80 l) for glaze firing – 40€
  • • Firing one piece only (cup size) bisque or glaze – 4€
