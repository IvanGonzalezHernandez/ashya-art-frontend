Open Studio

Welcome to Ashya’s Studio, your favourite place for all-around ceramics. Located in Hamburg, we offer a fun and welcoming environment for everyone, whether you’re a newbie or a pro. Try your hand at wheel throwing, hand building, and glazing with our awesome instructors. Come hang out, get creative, and make some amazing pottery with us!

After the Beginner Course

After completing the Hand Building Ceramic Class, you’ll be ready for our Open Studio! Here, you can apply and further develop what you’ve learned in a supportive and inspiring environment.

Every Wednesday and Friday from 6 PM to 9 PM and Saturday from 4:30 PM to 8 PM, our studio is open for you to practice independently and enhance your skills. Experiment with different types of clay and techniques while enjoying the creative community.

  • Level: Experienced level required (or completion of at least one hand-building class at Ashya Studio).
  • Materials included: 2kg of clay
  • Price: 30€ per session
  • Firing: Not included (see firing service for costs)
