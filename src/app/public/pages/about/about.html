About Ashya

Where tradition meets contemporary design — crafted with care, shared with soul.

Our Story

Ashya was founded in Hamburg with the goal of reviving traditional ceramic craftsmanship with a modern twist. Our passion is to create pieces that are not only functional but also tell a story — blending heritage, art, and everyday beauty.

Sustainability and quality are at the core of our work. We carefully select eco-friendly materials and collaborate with local artisans to keep the craft alive.

Workshops & Courses

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced artist, our workshops offer a welcoming space to learn and explore ceramic arts. From wheel throwing to glazing techniques, we tailor courses to nurture creativity and skill.

Join our community of makers, and let’s craft something beautiful together.

Community & Shop

Ashya is more than a studio — it's a community. We host regular events, open studio days, and exhibitions. Discover our shop featuring exclusive handmade ceramics perfect for gifting or elevating your space.

Stay connected with us through social media and newsletters for the latest news and creations.
